Hello from the Magic Tavern

After falling through a portal behind a Burger King in Chicago on Earth, Arnie Niekamp found himself in the magical land of Foon. Using the wi-fi signal from the Burger King, Arnie along with his Foonian friends, Chunt the shapeshifter and Usidore the wizard, host a weekly podcast during which they interview residents of Foon. Guests range from all sorts of magical creatures from a mouse human strength and a foul-mouthed flower to a necromancer made of 1,000 snakes in a skin suit, the Barron of Shrike Valley, and his creepy twins.

Immerse yourself into a fantastical world, get to know the stories of Foon's diverse characters, see if you can keep up with the pop curlture references, and revel (or maybe blush) in some good humored silliness.

Hello from the Hosts

Arnie Niekamp

Arnie is a human from Chicago, Illinois on Earth. He fell through a dimensional portal behind the Burger King on the corner of Irving Park and Clark Street in Chicago and landed in the magical land of Foon. Since he had his podcasting equipment in his Toyota Camry, which fell through the portal with him, Arnie decided to start a podcast with his new friends, Chunt and Usidore, using the Burger King wi-fi. He is known as the guy from another world, and he is bad at math.

Nicknames: Carnival Wilson, Fat Shaggy, Nie Nie

Played by Arnie Niekamp

Chunt the Shapeshifter

Chunt is a shapeshifter who can often be found in badger form. The child of a hunger ghost and a manticore (a creature with a lion's head, scorpion body, and dragon or bat wings), Chunt is a popular staple of Foon known for his various ventures such as hosting Chunt's Night and running Chu-Chu's Chow.

Nicknames: Chu Chu, King of the Badger, Li'l Squish

Played by Adal Rifai

Usidore the Blue

Usidore is the Blue wizard. He was created of the earth, wind, fire, and birds and came into the world as a fully formed adult over 300 years ago. Having studied at the Great Halls of Tarr'akkas, he devotes his life to defeating evil. Most noteably he seeks to defeat "The Dark Lord." He has many names including secret ones that are said to have great, and sometimes destructive, power.

Some of Usidore's names: Usidore the Blue, Wizard of the 12th Realm of Ephysiyies, Master of Light and Shadow, Manipulator of Magical Delights, Devourer of Chaos, Champion of the Great Halls of Terr'akkas, the elves know him as Fi’ang Yalok, the dwarfs know him as Zoenen Hoogstandjes, and he is known in the Northeast as Gaismunēnas Meistar, and there may be other secret names you do not know yet

Learn more about Usidore and his names on his website Usidore ROCKS!

Played by Matt Young
